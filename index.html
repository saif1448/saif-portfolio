About Me

As a dedicated Computer Science educator and experienced Software Developer, I bring a unique blend of academic insight and practical software engineering expertise. With over 6 years of university-level and online teaching experience, I have designed and delivered comprehensive courses in Java, Python, and core computer science subjects, empowering students to develop industry-ready skills.

Concurrently, I have accumulated 4+ years of hands-on experience in developing enterprise-grade software and RESTful APIs, currently leading in-house software projects at Janata Bank PLC using Java Spring Boot. My academic research in Natural Language Processing, particularly on BERT-based models, showcases my commitment to advancing the field through AI and ML.

I am passionate about building impactful solutions, mentoring aspiring technologists, and continuously learning to bridge the gap between education and innovation.
